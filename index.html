Cisco Networking Academy Microsites

Cisco Systems

Summary

This was a great project, I was part of the Cisco DemandGen Team for Corporate Affairs. We were tasked with developing a tool that would allow users to sign up for a pilot progam for several self-service e-learning products.
I was able to develop several personas that were used for an initial baseline. I created several iterations of wireframes, and adjusted them as I did user testing across the various versions. I then created high resolution mockups with a style guide that was used by the front end developers.
We had a goal of getting 3500 signups in the first 3 months, we were able to far exceed this by acquiring new 11,000 users for the platform. It was a tremendous success for Cisco.

Cisco Networking Academy Website UX/UI Design

Cisco Systems

Summary

This was a very large undertaking but so rewarding, the site had over 1 million monthly unique users. The Cisco Netacad site design had not been updated for serveral years and had large issues around site complexity with over 600 unique pages.
First was a series of interviews with stakeholders to understand the goal of the site. Through various types of research I was able to understand who the site was for, which allowed me to develop 1 primary and 7 secondary personas. This helped me understand what content on the site was antiquated and reduce its complexity down to just over 30 pages.
I conducted several series of user testing including card sorting, unmoderated and moderated testing of iterations of wireframes. Through this testing I was able iterate and land on a final high resolution design with a style guide to hand off to the front end developers.
The goal was to increase user engagement by 5%, the new design was able to perform a 10% increase.
